Geometa

Geometa is a video game developer and producer located in Leamington Spa, Warwickshire, of the United Kingdom.
Incorporated on the 23rd of January 2014, with a team of four employees, Geometa has developed 3 games.


Stormworks is a physics sandbox set on a massive oceanic map,
with a focus on sea and air rescue.


Carrier Command 2 is a sci-fi carrier mil-sim strategy game,
based on the original from 1988.


True Legacy is a text-based story game for the Iphone and Ipad.
