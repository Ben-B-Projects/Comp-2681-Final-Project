Stormworks Tutorials & Info

Electric Motors

Motors use electricity to produce rotational force.

Connect electricity, attach a pipe to it's output power, and send a number between -1 to 1 and the motor will try to spin at -20 to 20 rps to match this.

Pump

Pumps use electricity to push liquids or gases.

Connect electricity, attach the ringed side in the direction of output flow, then send a boolean signal to enable it.

Prebuilt Engine

Prebuilt engines use air, diesel, cooling, and produce rotational force and exhaust.

Connect electricity to run it's starter, connect air & diesel to their inputs, and connect a cooling solution to the cooling input and output.

Once the inputs are connected, connect exhaust to both outputs, and ensure it is piped to outside air.

To control the engine, send a boolean signal to run the starter, and send a number between 0 to 1 to control the throttle which changes the amount of fuel and air being used to produce power.

Microcontrollers

Microcontrollers are extremely customizable controllers for to control boolean, number, composite, audio, and visual signals.

Start by opening the MC editor, name your new controller, and add nodes for each input and output you'll need, then adjust the nodes to fit seperately on the schematic.

Open the logic editor, seperate out each node, then using the logic gate inventory place down the gates you need and attach them up as necessary. Once you save your MC you can use it from your editor inventory
